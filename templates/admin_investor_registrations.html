Investor Verification Process

Follow these steps to verify and approve investor accounts:

  1. Review - Check the investor's details including name, email, mobile and PAN
  2. Verification - For investors with failed PAN verification, upload supporting documents
  3. Approval/Rejection - Approve verified investors or reject with reason

Document Upload: You can upload verification documents (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC) for manual verification when automated PAN verification fails.
